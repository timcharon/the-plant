return

(Flugzeug landet)

2019

interviews and publication
with Sven Krahl

Spending a whole day at Frankfurt Airport. Not in the terminal or on the tarmac, but on a concrete platform at the other end of the many fences. And photographing aeroplanes. (Flugzeug landet) accompanies such a day at the Frankfurt Airport and sheds light on different perspectives on the hobby of planespotting and on collecting all around the aircraft.
